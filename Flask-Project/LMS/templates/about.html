About Our Library Management System

Our Mission

Our Library Management System aims to simplify and modernize the way libraries manage their collections, memberships, and transactions. We believe in empowering librarians and readers with technology that promotes efficient book circulation and easy access to knowledge.

Key Features

Book Management

Easily add, update, and categorize books for efficient tracking and availability.

Member Management

Maintain accurate records of library members, their profiles, and borrowing history.

Automated Issue & Return

Quickly issue and return books with automatic due date tracking and fine calculation.

Reports & Analytics

Generate detailed reports on book usage, member activity, and library performance.
